2000 Update to Actuarial Note 139

Top 1000 names of 2000

This update to Actuarial Note 139 presents the most frequently used given names for births in 2000. The data comes from a 100% sampling of Social Security card applications for births in 2000. This update replaces an earlier 1% sampling of births from January through August.

The total number of males in the sample is 2,089,457 and the total number of females is 1,996,763. Criteria to be included in the sample is simply that a Social Security card application was filed and that the year of birth was 2000. Birth on United States soil was not a requirement. As always each unique spelling is considered a unique name. It may be appropriate for purposes of ranking popularity of names to combine similar spellings of the same name. This kind of grouping, however, is subjective and time consuming, and is beyond the scope of this document.
